About

Nick Hodge is an accomplished Cyr Wheel & Aerial Rope performer. He is currently based out of Las Vegas, NV and performs nationwide. He was a featured artist at the North Carolina Museum of Art's 24 Year Retrospective, winner and headlining performer at the Minnesota State Fair's Amateur Talent Competition, and the main act for the San Antonio Spurs' Half-Time Show on New Year's Eve, 2019.

Nick teaches Cyr wheel classes, offers online coaching & private lessons, and travels for workshops. In competition, Nick placed 1st at the 2019 USA Wheel Gymnastics Federation's National Competition and 5th at the 2018 IRV Wheel Gymnastics World Championship.

Nick Hodge was recently accepted into the databases of Cirque du Soleil, The 7 Fingers, and Cirque Eloize. He is available for 2020-2021 contracts, shows, and workshops.
